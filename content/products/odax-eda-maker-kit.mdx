## Overview

The ODAX EDA Maker Kit bundles the core items needed to start working with electrodermal activity (EDA) and galvanic skin response (GSR) in a simple maker-oriented setup.

It combines the ODAX EDA Maker analog sensor board, reusable maker-grade ODAX finger contact straps, and electrode leads with a 3.5 mm TS audio jack connection so builders can move from parts sourcing to testing more quickly.

This kit is intended for prototyping, evaluation, biofeedback experiments, biometrics, HCI research, and exploratory embedded development. It is not a medical device and is not intended for diagnosis/treatment, or safety-critical use.

## Kit Contents

The kit includes 3 items:

1. **ODAX EDA Maker board**
2. **ODAX EDA Contact Strap - Maker Grade - Black**

3. **Electrode leads with 3.5 mm TS audio jack**
